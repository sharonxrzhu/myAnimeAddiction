Main Characters:

Conan Edogawa

Conan Edogawa is the child version of Shinichi Kudo. He was poisoned that turned him into a child by the Black Organization. His goal is to try to hunt them down and have them arrested for their crimes, as well as find an antidote to this poison. Shinichi Kudo is the lover of Ran Mouri, after he turned into a child he lives with the Mouris. He is extremly good at solving detective cases and helps their detective office to solve cases in the name of Ran's Dad. He has a cool bow that can imitate any voice he wants. "There is always only one truth." is a famous quote that he said.

Ran Mouri/Rachel Moore

Ran Mouri lives with her Dad Richard Mouri. She is the a high school student who is the childhood friend and later girlfriend of Shinichi Kudo. She is very good at martial arts.

Kogoro Mouri/Richard Moore

Kohoro Mouri is a private detective and former police officer. He gets drunk quite a lot and almost always shot to sleep by Conan so Conan could imitate him to give the solution to the case since people don't believe a little kid.
