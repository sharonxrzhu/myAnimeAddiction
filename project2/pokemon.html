Main Characters:

Ash Ketchum

Ash Ketchum is a 10-year-old Pokémon Trainer from Pallet Town in the Kanto region. Because he overslept, his first pokémon is a pikachu that was at first very uncontrolable. Through out their journey together they build friendship and started to trust each other. Their ultimate goal is becoming the world's greatest Pokémon Master.

Ash's Pikachu

This pikachu is Ash's first pokémon ever from professor Oak and becomes partners. Ash always brings pikachu while traveling to explore the pokémon world.
