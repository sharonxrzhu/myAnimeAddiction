Description:

There's a small shop named "Time Photo Gallery", it was run by Qiao Ling, Lu Guang and Cheng Xiaoshi. Lu Guang and Cheng Xiaoshi have special abilties to enter photos. To complete the costumer's needs, Cheng Xiaoshi can transfer into the phototaker, control and experience the next 12 hours of the photo's history using the phototaker's body, while Luguang can see what has happened to make sure Chengxiaoshi does not change the results of history, because changing history will change everything after in the future, and they try to avoid that from happening. They are partners. They can only transfer into each photo once, which means they only have one chance per photo. Their slogan is "no matter past or future, just let them be". However, they cannot always make sure nothing is changed in the future due to their actions, and things started to get out of their control.

Main Characters:

Lu Guang

He can see what will happen in the next 12 hours of a photo, and can see what the executor that transferred into the photo(Cheng Xiaoshi) see, through the action of clapping hands with him, in order to direct further movements. He is the partner of Cheng Xiaoshi, acts calmly, rationally, and is the role of director in their commissions.

Cheng Xiaoshi

He can transfer into the picture taker of a photo and control his/her actions for the next 12 hours. He is the partner of Lu Guang, he listens to his directions as he is in the photo. He is righteous, emotional, and impulsive. He is the executor in their commission. Sometimes he does not listen to Lu Guang's plan and things does not go so well.

Qiao Ling

She is the owner of their small shop, grew up with Cheng Xiaoshi together. She helps Cheng Xiaoshi and Lu Guang take commission.
